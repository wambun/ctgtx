# Goosehead Insurance

![](https://ctgtx.com/app/uploads/2020/11/logo-1.png)

Connect Technology Group was contracted to design Goosehead Insurance access control, audio/visual, white noise and structured cabling infrastructure. Audio/Video was required in multiple conference rooms, (1) boardroom and (1) companywide town hall meeting area. Access control system consisted of (20) doors. Installation of (3) telecommunications rooms with multiple cabinets/racks and over (450) category 6 cables.

**Strategic Partners:** The Siemon Company, GrayBar, Kantech, Cambridge, Triad, Atlona and Stampede

**Project budget:** $325,000

**Sales Representative:** Brad Heebsh

**Project Manager:** Denis Weers, Mike Moriarty,

**Lead Technician:** Jason Cervantes and John McGill

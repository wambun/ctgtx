# Nuvodesk

![](https://ctgtx.com/app/uploads/2020/11/logo.png)

Connect Technology Group was contracted for a design to build installation for Nuvodesk, a Coâ€“ working facility in Grand Prairie. This $400,000 project consisted of Security/ Audio/Visual and Structured Cabling. Security consisted of 20+ Access Control card readers and interior/ exterior secu- rity cameras. Audio/video included room schedulers, displays and sound masking. Structured Cabling consisted 200+ test, tone and labeled cables and a telecommunication room with a custom LED rack for an extra pop of color to match the bold Nuvodesk logo.

**Featured Products:**

OWLLabs Owl 360 video conferencing all in one camera, microphone, and speaker.

Custom LED lighting

Custom Aqua Cat 6 Cable & Fiber

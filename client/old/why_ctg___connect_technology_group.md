# Why CTG?

**Connect Technology Group is a team of dedicated and tenured professionals with a combined 40 years of professional installation experience. As a potential client, you can count on CTG as we are a proven, ready and trusted company. Connect Technology Group’s mission is to utilize our extensive market and industry experience and expertise to deliver concrete business results enabling our clients to profit from the advanced use of information transport systems and solutions. We strive to build long-term client relationships based on shared trust and respect. Our goal is to be the best in the eyes of our clients, employees and stake holders.**

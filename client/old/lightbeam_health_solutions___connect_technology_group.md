# Lightbeam Health Solutions

![](https://ctgtx.com/app/uploads/2023/12/lightbeam_logo__color-resized.png)

We provided comprehensive solutions for the installation of state-of-the-art security cameras, advanced access control systems, top-notch audio-video setups, reliable UPS systems, and cutting-edge network infrastructure for Lightbeam Health Solutions in Coppell, TX. Our expert team ensured seamless integration and optimal performance, guaranteeing utmost safety, efficiency, and convenience.

**Strategic Partners:** Siemon/ APC/ Atlona/ LG/ Digital Watchdog/ Brivo

**Project Budget:** $500,000

**Sales Representative:** Brad Heebsh

**Project Manager:** Mike Moriarty
